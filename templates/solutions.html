Women Safety & Empowerment Schemes

  • Chief Minister Kanya Sumangala Yojana: Supports girl child from birth to graduation. Official Link
  • Mission Shakti: Empowers women via awareness, legal & medical support. Official Website
  • Safe City Project: Surveillance & pink booths for urban safety. Read More
  • She-Box: Online complaint system for workplace sexual harassment. Report Here
  • One Stop Centres: Support for victims: legal, medical, shelter. District List
  • Women’s Shelters & Hostels: Shelter with legal/vocational support. Explore
  • Legal Aid: Free legal help for dowry/domestic violence cases. Apply
